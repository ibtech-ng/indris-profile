BOXCAR


A Travel Agency Looking To Give Elegant Experience To Women When Travelling

Challenge

A starting travel agency that want to have a unique voice and compelling identity to communicate with their audience and give path to great expansionvoice.

Project Structure

brand identity, visual identity, logo.

Approach

Women do live questionable lifestyles so we did lots of researches on how women live through various way of life, we then compare it to the brand mission, on how it will fit and if its going to work nice to get where they need to be, elegance is a key, we gave the brand an identity base on the structure and simplicity for easy assimilation of the brand voice.
